Research Experience

  • Self-Organizing Behavior of Multi-Agent Systems
  By leveraging information sharing and collaboration among individuals, multi-agent systems are expected to achieve high-level swarm intelligence, applicable to more complex application scenarios. For the distributed state estimation and control issues of multi-agent systems, how to realize the self-organization design and implementation of individuals for estimation and control strategies is not only of theoretical significance, but also of practical values. For this issue, we have developed a class of self-organizing control frameworks for multi-agent systems.
